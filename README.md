**Harmonia Christi** is a choral ensemble dedicated to the beauty of sacred music and choral excellence. Founded on the principles of community and faith, our choir brings together voices to perform a diverse repertoire ranging from classical religious masterpieces to modern liturgical works.

We believe that music is a bridge to the spiritual, and through our performances, we strive to inspire, comfort, and uplift our audience.

- **Choral Performances**: Regular appearances at liturgical services and special events.

- **Vocal Training**: Developing the technical and artistic skills of our members.
- **Community Building**: Creating a supportive environment for singers of all backgrounds.

## üíª About This Site
This platform serves as a **Digital Resource Library** created specifically for the members of the Harmonia Christi Choir. It is designed to modernize how we manage our repertoire and prepare for performances.

**Key Features:**
- **Centralized Partitures**: Instant access to PDF scores for the entire repertoire.
- **Voice-Specific Audio Paths**: High-quality audio guides for Soprano, Alto, Tenor, and Bass to facilitate individual practice.
- **Concert Management**: Dedicated views for upcoming concert programs.
- **Responsive Design**: Accessible from any device, whether you're in a rehearsal hall or practicing at home.
